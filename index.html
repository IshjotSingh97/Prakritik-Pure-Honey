Honey

Honey is a sweet viscous food substance naturally made by Honey Bees. They produce honey from sugary secretions of floral nectar. Bees store honey in waxed structures called honeycombs. Honey is collected from wild bee colonies, or from hives of domesticated bees, a practice known as Beekeeping.Honey gets its sweetness from the monosaccharides fructose glucose and sucrose.
